Course Overview

In 2025, your personal brand is your new resume. This course teaches you how to intentionally build a professional online presence that showcases your expertise, values, and skills. We'll move beyond "posting online" and teach you a strategy to build authority, network effectively, and have recruiters and clients come to *you*.

What You Will Learn

  • How to define your niche and unique value proposition.
  • Optimizing your LinkedIn profile to become a "Talent Magnet."
  • Fundamentals of content creation (writing, simple graphics, short video).
  • Building a simple one-page portfolio to showcase your work.
  • Strategies for networking online without being salesy.
  • Managing your online reputation and digital footprint.

Course Structure

Weeks 1-2: Foundation (Defining Your Brand, LinkedIn Mastery)

Weeks 3-4: Content & Creation (Content Strategy, Writing for Web, Canva Basics)

Weeks 5-6: Networking & Portfolio (Building a Portfolio, Networking Strategy, Final Project)

Who Should Join
