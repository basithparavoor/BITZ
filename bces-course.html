BCES — Bitz Certified Course in Essential Soft Skills

Build the Skills That Build Your Success.

Duration: 3 Months Mode: Hybrid

Course Overview

BCES (Bitz Certified Course in Essential Soft Skills) is a practical, industry-focused program that equips learners with the interpersonal abilities and workplace behaviours employers value most. This foundation course develops communication, emotional intelligence, teamwork, professional etiquette, time & stress management, and leadership fundamentals through hands-on workshops, real-world projects and continuous feedback.

The BCES curriculum is activity-led — expect role-plays, simulations, group projects and a final capstone that simulates a workplace challenge. Our approach ensures learners can immediately apply skills on the job or in interviews.

What You Will Learn

  • Communication Mastery: Clear speaking, persuasive conversations, and confident presentations.
  • Emotional Intelligence (EQ): Self-awareness, empathy, and emotional regulation in professional settings.
  • Teamwork & Collaboration: Effective collaboration, conflict resolution and productive meetings.
  • Professional Etiquette: Email & report writing, workplace behaviour, and professional grooming.
  • Problem Solving & Decision Making: Simple frameworks for fast, effective decisions.
  • Leadership & Influence: Leading small teams, giving feedback, and motivating others.
  • Time & Stress Management: Prioritisation, planning and maintaining performance under pressure.

Course Structure

Week 1 — Communication Essentials: Listening skills, speaking structure, short presentations and interpersonal conversation practice.

Week 2 — Workplace Professionalism: Business email writing, professional presence, etiquette and mock interviews.

Week 3 — Emotional & Social Intelligence: Managing emotions, empathy exercises, role-play for conflict resolution and team dynamics.

Week 4 — Leadership, Problem Solving & Capstone: Decision-making frameworks, micro-leadership tasks, final group capstone project and assessment.

Capstone Project: Each group will present a workplace-scenario solution (project plan + pitch) followed by peer & trainer evaluation.

Certification & Recognition

On successful completion and assessment, participants will receive the Bitz Certified Course in Essential Soft Skills (BCES) certificate. This credential is designed for placement assistance and can be shared on LinkedIn and resumes. BCES is backed by Bitz industry partners and recognized for entry-level hiring & workplace readiness.
