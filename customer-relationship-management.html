Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Master the art of customer service and retention.

Duration: 4 Weeks Mode: Hybrid Next Batch: Starts Nov 25th

Course Overview

In a competitive market, the most successful businesses are those that build strong customer relationships. This course teaches you the strategies and tools to manage the entire customer lifecycle, from lead to loyal advocate. You will learn practical customer service skills, client retention strategies, and the fundamentals of using CRM software.

What You Will Learn

  • The principles of modern Customer Relationship Management.
  • Advanced communication skills for handling difficult customers.
  • Strategies for customer onboarding, support, and retention.
  • How to use CRM software (e.g., HubSpot/Salesforce basics) to track interactions.
  • Measuring customer satisfaction (NPS, CSAT) and gathering feedback.
  • Techniques for upselling and cross-selling to existing clients.

Course Structure

Week 1: CRM Fundamentals (The Customer Lifecycle, Service Principles)

Week 2: Communication & Service (Handling Complaints, Active Listening)

Week 3: Tools & Technology (Intro to CRM Platforms, Managing Databases)

Week 4: Retention & Growth (Client Retention, Feedback & Metrics, Final Project)

Who Should Join

  • Aspiring customer service representatives or client success managers.
  • Sales and marketing professionals.
  • Small business owners and freelancers.
