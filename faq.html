Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs.

How long are your courses?

Most of our short-term skill courses range between 2 weeks and 12 weeks. The exact duration for each program is listed on its dedicated course detail page.

Do you provide certification?

Yes, all our major programs conclude with a final assessment. Upon successful completion, you will receive a recognized certification from Bitz. Some courses may also offer industry-partnered or national-level certifications. Please check the individual course page for details.

Are classes online or offline?

We offer Online, Offline (at our campus), and Hybrid (a mix of both) modes. The available modes depend on the specific course and batch. You can filter courses by mode on our Courses page.

Do you help with placements?

Yes. We provide comprehensive placement assistance, which includes internship support, resume building workshops, mock interviews, and connecting you with our network of industry partners. Our goal is to make you 100% job-ready.

What are the payment options?

We accept all major payment methods, including UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Netbanking. We also offer flexible EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) plans for eligible courses to make learning more affordable.

Can I get a demo class?

We regularly host free workshops and webinars that act as demo classes. Please check our Events page for the upcoming schedule, or contact our admissions team to book a counselling call.
