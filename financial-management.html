Financial Management (for Beginners)

Master budgeting, investing, and financial planning.

Duration: 4 Weeks Mode: Online Next Batch: Starts Nov 18th

Course Overview

This course is designed to demystify money for young professionals. We provide practical, real-world financial literacy that you can apply immediately. You will learn how to create a budget that actually works, how to save effectively, the basics of investing (stocks, mutual funds), and how to plan for your long-term financial goals.

What You Will Learn

  • The 50/30/20 budgeting rule and how to customize it.
  • Building an emergency fund and setting financial goals.
  • Understanding different investment options (Mutual Funds, Stocks, FDs).
  • The basics of income tax saving (80C) and financial planning.
  • How to read a payslip and manage your first salary.
  • Understanding debt, loans, and credit scores.

Course Structure

Week 1: Budgeting & Saving (Tracking Expenses, 50/30/20 Rule, Emergency Funds)

Week 2: Intro to Investing (Mutual Funds, SIPs, Stock Market Basics)

Week 3: Debt & Credit (Understanding Loans, Credit Score, Good vs. Bad Debt)

Week 4: Planning & Taxes (Goal Setting, Tax Saving Instruments, Final Plan)

Who Should Join

  • Young professionals who just started their first job.
  • Students who want to build good financial habits early.
  • Anyone who feels overwhelmed by money and wants a simple plan.
