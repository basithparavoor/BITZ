Employability Preparation Skills (EPS)

The complete toolkit for landing your dream job.

Duration: 3 Weeks Mode: Hybrid Next Batch: Starts Nov 10th

Course Overview

This is an intensive, action-oriented course designed to bridge the gap between your academic qualifications and the demands of the modern workplace. We provide the *exact* skills recruiters look for. You won't just learn theory; you will build a professional resume, practice interviews with mentors, and master corporate communication.

What You Will Learn

  • Crafting a keyword-optimized, ATS-friendly resume and cover letter.
  • Mastering the STAR method for behavioral interview questions.
  • Techniques for Group Discussions and how to make your point effectively.
  • Professional email etiquette and corporate communication.
  • How to research a company and ask intelligent questions in an interview.
  • Grooming, body language, and professional conduct.

Course Structure

Week 1: Building Your Profile (Resume Writing, LinkedIn Optimization, Cover Letters)

Week 2: Interview Mastery (Mock Interviews, STAR Method, Body Language, Grooming)

Week 3: Corporate Readiness (Group Discussions, Email Etiquette, Final Assessments)

Who Should Join

  • Final-year students and fresh graduates preparing for placements.
  • Anyone re-entering the job market after a break.
  • Professionals who want to improve their interview and communication skills.
