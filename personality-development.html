Personality Development

Boost confidence, master communication, and own every room.

Duration: 2 Weeks Mode: Hybrid

Course Overview

This course is designed for students, professionals, and anyone looking to enhance their personal and professional image. We focus on the core pillars of personality: communication, body language, etiquette, and self-confidence. You will move from being a hesitant speaker to a confident presenter.

Our methodology is 90% practical, involving mock interviews, group discussions, and role-playing scenarios to build real-world skills.

What You Will Learn

  • Mastering public speaking and presentation skills.
  • Effective body language and non-verbal cues.
  • Corporate etiquette and professional grooming.
  • Techniques for building lasting self-confidence.
  • Handling group discussions and interviews.
  • Active listening and interpersonal skills.

Course Structure

1: The Foundation (Self-Awareness, Goal Setting, Grooming)

2: Communication Mastery (Verbal, Non-Verbal, Public Speaking)

3: Professional Excellence (Etiquette, Mock Interviews, Resume)

4: Advanced Skills (Group Discussions, NLP Intro, Final Projects)
